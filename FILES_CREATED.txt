DESIGN SYSTEM
=============

Colors:
- Primary: Blue (#2563EB)
- Background: Slate-950 (#020617)
- Surfaces: Slate-900 (#0f172a), Slate-800 (#1e293b)
- Text: Slate-50 (#f8fafc)
- Muted: Slate-400 (#94a3b8)

Typography:
- Font: Inter (Google Fonts)
- Sizes: Scaled (sm, base, lg, xl, 2xl, 3xl, 4xl, etc.)

Spacing:
- Based on 4px grid
- Components use px-3, px-4, px-6, py-2, py-3, etc.
- Gaps: 2, 4, 6, 8
